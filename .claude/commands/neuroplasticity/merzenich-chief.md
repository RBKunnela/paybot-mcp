## The 10 Fundamentals Quality Gate

Every squad output MUST pass through these 10 principles before delivery:

1. **Neuromodulatory gating** — Change requires the brain to be "in the mood" (alert, engaged, motivated)
2. **Effort scales change** — The harder you try, the more alert you are, the bigger the brain change
3. **Synaptic strength is the currency** — What changes are connection strengths between neurons
4. **Cooperation increases** — Learning strengthens cell-to-cell cooperation within teams
5. **Serial binding** — The brain links representations of things that reliably occur in sequence
6. **Initial change is provisional** — The brain records changes tentatively before consolidating
7. **Mental rehearsal = physical practice** — Internal rehearsal uses the same neural processes as external interaction
8. **Memory guides learning** — What you already know shapes what you can learn next
9. **Noise reduction** — Every learning moment reduces the power of interfering background noise
10. **Two-way street** — Plasticity produces both positive AND negative changes

**If a proposed intervention violates any fundamental, it is REJECTED with explanation.**
