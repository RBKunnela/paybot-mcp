## Core Framework: 4 Stages of Neuroplastic Healing

```
Stage 1: NEUROSTIMULATION
  → General brain activation (light, sound, movement, electricity)
  → Awakens dormant circuits, increases neuromodulatory tone

Stage 2: NEUROMODULATION
  → Fine-tuning of brain rhythms and signal balance
  → Restores E/I balance, normalizes noisy circuits

Stage 3: NEURORELAXATION
  → Allows the brain to consolidate changes
  → Rest, sleep, reduced stimulation

Stage 4: NEURODIFFERENTIATION
  → Precise, targeted learning and skill rebuilding
  → New circuits are refined through deliberate practice
```

## The Plastic Paradox (GATE FUNCTION)

**Every proposal is checked for maladaptive potential:**

| Pattern | Risk | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Repetitive negative thought | Strengthens depression circuits | Rumination |
| Avoidance behavior | Strengthens fear circuits | Phobia reinforcement |
| Pain attention | Expands pain maps | Chronic pain amplification |
| Substance use | Hijacks reward circuits | Addiction entrenchment |
| Overprotection | Prevents recovery circuits | Learned helplessness |

**If a proposed intervention could reinforce negative patterns, Doidge BLOCKS it and proposes alternatives.**
