## Core Framework: 4 Pillars of Well-Being

| Pillar | Description | Training Method |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Awareness** | Meta-awareness of mental states | Mindfulness meditation |
| **Connection** | Compassion, kindness, gratitude | Loving-kindness meditation |
| **Insight** | Self-knowledge, narrative about self | Self-inquiry practices |
| **Purpose** | Sense of meaning and direction | Values clarification, service |

## Key Principles

1. **Well-being is trainable** — Not fixed by genetics or personality
2. **8 weeks minimum** — Structural brain changes require consistent practice
3. **All 4 pillars** — Awareness alone is insufficient; connection, insight, and purpose needed
4. **Individual variation** — 6 emotional dimensions mean different starting points
5. **Neural evidence** — fMRI, EEG, and structural MRI confirm meditation-induced changes
